About NAQI

بِسْمِ اللهِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

Noor Al-Qur’an Institute (NAQI) was informally founded on 1st August 2025 by Salah Abdullah, who also serves as the primary instructor. The institute was established with the noble mission of imparting the Qur’an with mastery, focusing on tadabbur (reflection) and tajwīd (proper recitation). NAQI was conceived to provide flexible and accessible Qur’anic learning opportunities, particularly for college students, working adults, and lifelong learners striving to balance worldly responsibilities with spiritual growth.

Origins and Divine Mission: Guided by the principles of ilm (knowledge) and ikhlas (sincerity), NAQI nurtures students’ connection with the Qur’an, combining rigorous academic learning with practical accessibility. The founder, Salah Abdullah, personally mentors students to help them achieve clarity, consistency, and mastery in Qur’anic reading, memorization, and Tajweed.

Faith-Driven Intention (Niyyah)

NAQI was established with a heartfelt niyyah to serve the Ummah by spreading Qur’anic knowledge and fostering Qur’an-centered character (akhlaq). The institute’s ethos extends beyond teaching to inspire students to embody the Qur’an in daily life, cultivating mindfulness, ethical conduct, and spiritual excellence.

Humble Beginnings

At its inception, NAQI offered online classes exclusively via Google Meet, scheduled on weekends to accommodate students and working professionals. The institute began modestly, welcoming its first student with an emphasis on personalized, high-quality instruction. This approach laid the foundation for a learning environment rooted in care, dedication, and barakah (divine blessing).

Vision for Expansion (Ru’yah)

While currently serving students in the United States, NAQI envisions future growth and outreach across North America and, by the will of Allah, globally. Expansion plans include additional qualified instructors and administrative staff to uphold NAQI’s mission: delivering flexible, high-quality Qur’anic education while nurturing spiritual development and community engagement.

Curriculum Tracks

Noor Al-Qur’an Institute (NAQI) offers a structured and flexible Qur’anic curriculum designed to accommodate students of varying levels, goals, and capacities. The curriculum emphasizes clarity, consistency, and mastery while allowing students to pursue Qur’anic studies alongside academic and professional commitments.

Reading Track

Purpose: Develop Qur’an reading fluency without memorization or advanced Tajweed study.

This Track Is Suitable For:

  • Beginners learning to read the Qur’an
  • Students improving pronunciation and fluency
  • Individuals who prefer reading over memorization

Core Focus:

  • Arabic letter recognition
  • Proper pronunciation
  • Reading fluency and accuracy

Add-On Option: Tajweed Track (optional)

Memorization Track

Purpose: Memorize portions of the Qur’an according to the student’s capacity and pace.

This Track Is Suitable For:

  • Students committed to memorizing the Qur’an
  • Learners with basic reading ability
  • Individuals seeking structured memorization and revision

Core Focus:

  • Guided memorization plans
  • Revision and retention strategies
  • Accuracy during recitation

Add-On Option: Tajweed Track (optional)

Note: Qur’an reading is inherently included in this track.

Tajweed Track

Purpose: Study the science of Qur’anic recitation (ʿIlm al-Tajwīd) and apply it correctly.

This Track Is Suitable For:

  • Students seeking to understand Tajweed rules
  • Individuals refining recitation accuracy
  • Learners interested in the scholarly foundations of recitation

Core Areas of Study:

  • Makharij al-Ḥurūf (articulation points)
  • Ṣifāt al-Ḥurūf (letter characteristics)
  • Foundational Tajweed rules

Primary Text: تيسير أحكام التجويد (Taysīr Ahkām Al-Tajwīd) – a beginner-friendly yet comprehensive text providing clarity and depth for foundational and intermediate Tajweed.

Practical Application Requirement:

  • Tajweed with Qur’an Reading
  • Tajweed with Qur’an Memorization
Track Combination Policy:
  • Students enroll in one primary track.
  • Tajweed may be taken:
    • As an add-on to Reading or Memorization, or
    • As a solo primary track
  • Reading and Memorization cannot be combined as dual primary tracks.

Pricing & Payment Structure

NAQI offers a flexible track-based payment structure. Students enroll in one primary track and may optionally add an additional track to enhance their learning experience.

  • Tajweed may be added as an optional add-on to either Reading or Memorization.

NAQI’s approach combines structured lesson plans with flexible scheduling, enabling students to progress at their own pace while maintaining consistency and dedication.
