Curriculum Tracks

NAQI offers a structured and flexible Qur’anic curriculum emphasizing clarity, mastery, and consistency.

Reading Track

Purpose: Develop reading fluency.

  • Beginners learning to read the Qur’an
  • Improve pronunciation and fluency
  • Prefer reading over memorization

Memorization Track

Purpose: Memorize portions of the Qur’an.

  • Committed to memorization
  • Basic reading ability required
  • Structured memorization and revision

Tajweed Track

Purpose: Study Qur’anic recitation rules (ʿIlm al-Tajwīd).

  • Understand Tajweed rules
  • Refine recitation accuracy
  • Scholarly foundations of recitation
Track Combination Policy:
  • One primary track per student
  • Tajweed optional add-on
  • Reading & Memorization cannot be combined
