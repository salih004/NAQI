Pricing & Payment Structure

NAQI offers a flexible track-based payment structure. Students enroll in one primary track and may optionally add an additional track.

Enrollment Option Monthly Tuition
First Track (any primary track) $100
Each Additional Track (add-on) +$50
Payment Guidelines:
  • Monthly payments are billed based on the number of tracks currently enrolled.
  • If a student changes tracks mid-month, the same monthly fee applies.
  • Reading and Memorization cannot be combined as dual primary tracks.
  • All three tracks cannot be taken together.
  • Tajweed may be added as an optional add-on to either Reading or Memorization.
